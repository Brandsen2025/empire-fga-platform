Quick Summary

He was a true journeyman goalscorer, finding success in Colombia, Argentina, Peru, and Mexico, with over 190 career goals.

Player Overview

Martín Enrique Arzuaga Coronel, "El Torito," was a classic #9, a powerful and prolific centre-forward. He is a club legend at Junior de Barranquilla, where he had multiple stints and was the hero of the 2004 Torneo Finalización, scoring in the final and converting the championship-winning penalty. His career was defined by goals, including a record-breaking 22-goal season for Uniautónoma in 2013. He was a true journeyman, playing in Colombia, Mexico, Argentina, and Peru, and also earned 10 caps for the Colombian national team, scoring once.

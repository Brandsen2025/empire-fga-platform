Analysis

Lokembo operated with a "survival of the fittest" mentality. In the physical battles of the Cypriot First Division, he thrived on contact. He was not a modern ball-playing defender; he was a stopper who took pride in clearing lines and winning aerial duels. His psychological profile suggests a player who valued structure and discipline, executing his defensive duties with high intensity but sometimes lacking the cool head required for build-up play.

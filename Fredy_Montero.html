Player Overview

Fredy Henkyer Montero Muñoz is one of the most successful and iconic players in Seattle Sounders and MLS history. He began at Deportivo Cali and was a two-time league top scorer in Colombia (with Huila and Cali) before joining Seattle as an inaugural Designated Player in 2009. He became an instant star, winning MLS Newcomer of the Year and being named an MLS All-Star 3 times. He is the club's all-time leading goalscorer (79 goals). Montero also had a highly successful career at Sporting CP in Portugal, winning the Taça de Portugal and two Taça da Liga cups. He won the 2022 CONCACAF Champions League in his second stint with Seattle.

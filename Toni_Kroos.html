Player Overview

Toni Kroos is a German legend and one of the most decorated players in football history, renowned for his "exquisite technique, remarkable vision and passing precision." A quintessential deep-lying playmaker, he dictated the tempo for a decade at Real Madrid, making 465 appearances. His career is defined by unparalleled success: he won the 2014 FIFA World Cup with Germany and a record 6 Champions League titles (one with Bayern Munich, five with Real Madrid). He also won 6 FIFA Club World Cups, 4 La Liga titles, and 3 Bundesliga titles, accumulating 32 major club trophies.

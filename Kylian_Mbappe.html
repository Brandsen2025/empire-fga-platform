Player Overview

Kylian Mbappé Lottin is arguably the most electrifying attacker in world football, defined by his truly world-class speed (SPR 10.0) and devastating ability to beat defenders (DXT, CRK, FLA). He combines this physical and technical prowess with exceptional finishing (GOL, STK, RDS) and increasingly effective link-up play (KEY, SCA). Capable of playing centrally or wide left, his direct threat and consistent goal output make him a generational talent and perennial Ballon d'Or contender.

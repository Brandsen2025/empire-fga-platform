Player Overview

Wilmar Santiago Londoño Ruiz is a retired Colombian goalkeeper who came from the renowned Envigado FC academy, "The Cantera of Heroes." He made his professional debut in 2015 and served as a reliable keeper for the club for seven seasons, accumulating 123 total appearances. Known for his agility and personality, his busiest seasons were in 2018 (26 starts) and 2020 (17 starts). He moved to Deportivo Pereira for the 2023 season before retiring in early 2024.

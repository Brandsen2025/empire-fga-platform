Player Overview

Wei Xiangxin is carrying the hopes of a nation on his shoulders. Hailed as the striker "China needs," he is a strong, composed, and highly confident forward who models his game and mentality on his hero, Cristiano Ronaldo (he has already "shown his abs to local media"). He joined Meizhou Hakka at the start of 2025 and immediately made history, becoming the first player born in 2008 to play in the Chinese Super League, then scoring his first goal in his second match. Despite his club's likely relegation, Wei's future is bright. He has already trialed with Auxerre and is eager for a permanent move to Europe.

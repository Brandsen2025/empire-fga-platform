Analysis

Bobby Moore's psychological profile is characterized by utter tranquility under extreme pressure. He never looked flustered, sweaty, or panicked. His mental processing speed allowed him to see developing plays seconds before anyone else. He was a quiet leader who commanded respect through excellence rather than shouting. His ability to wipe his hands before shaking the Queen's hand after winning the World Cup exemplifies his presence of mind.

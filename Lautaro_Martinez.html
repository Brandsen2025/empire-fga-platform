Player Overview

Lautaro Martínez represents the modern complete forward - a player who scores prolifically while contributing defensively with tireless pressing and work rate. Since joining Inter Milan in 2018, he has developed into one of Europe's most feared strikers, winning the Scudetto and Capocannoniere (top scorer). His role in Argentina's 2022 World Cup triumph and multiple Copa América victories showcases his ability to deliver in the biggest moments. Despite standing at 1.74m, his explosive acceleration, intelligent movement, and clinical finishing make him a nightmare for defenders. Now Inter's captain, he embodies the club's winning mentality.

Player Overview

Leon Jakirovic is one of the most intelligent and versatile defensive prospects in the Balkans. A rare left-footed player, he possesses a high-level "reading and understanding of the game" that allows him to play three different positions: centre-back, full-back, and defensive midfielder. His early coach, Ivan Mijac, noted this rare versatility. He was given his senior debut for Dinamo Zagreb by his father, Sergej Jakirovic, in May 2024, and is a key defensive partner to Raul Kumar in Croatia's youth teams. His intelligence and valuable left foot make him a prized asset for Dinamo and a target for major European clubs.

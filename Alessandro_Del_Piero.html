Analysis

Del Piero represents the "Gentleman" archetype in football. He was rarely involved in controversy and led by example. His psychological strength was tested when he suffered a career-altering knee injury in 1998; he reinvented his game from explosive dribbler to technical mastermind. His decision to stay with Juventus in Serie B (2006) despite being a fresh World Cup winner demonstrates a level of loyalty rarely seen in modern sports.
